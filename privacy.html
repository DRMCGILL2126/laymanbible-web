Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Layman Bible ("we," "our," "the site") handles information when you visit laymanbible.com.

Information We Collect

Layman Bible does not require an account and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. The site stores a small amount of data locally in your browser (via localStorage) to remember your preferences, such as:

  • Font size
  • Light or dark theme
  • Which explainer toggles (chapter context, cultural notes, cross-references, etc.) you have enabled

This data stays on your device and is never sent to us.

Cookies and Advertising

We use Google AdSense to show ads on this site. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can learn more about how Google uses data and manage your ad personalization settings at Google's Ads Policy page.

Because this site serves ads through Google, third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites on the Internet.

Analytics

We may use standard, privacy-respecting analytics (such as aggregate page-view counts) to understand which parts of the site are useful. This data is anonymized and not tied to your identity.

Children's Privacy

Layman Bible is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

You can clear your locally stored preferences at any time by clearing your browser's site data for laymanbible.com. You can also opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ad Settings or via the Digital Advertising Alliance.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
